Vibes & Experiences

Velvet Horizon is more than a dining destination — it’s a vibrant universe where music, laughter, and friendship converge. Inside, you’ll find cozy corners for couples seeking a romantic dinner, lively areas where friends gather to play cards, share stories, and enjoy a drink, and an open lounge where our resident DJ sets the tone for the night with deep house and lounge beats. Our connected venues extend the experience — a private bar for signature cocktails, an outdoor terrace for starry evenings, and a game lounge designed for pure fun and social moments. Whether you seek energy or intimacy, Velvet Horizon has a place for every mood.
